Accessibility
We at Stepik are committed to maintaining access to our website and mobile applications to all of our learners, including those with disabilities.

Accessibility has been important to Stepik since the day it was launched.

To achieve our goal of accessibility for all learners, we are committed to the following:

  • Stepik aims to conform with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines ("WCAG") 2.0 AA published by World Wide Web Consortium.
  • Content on Stepik is available to learners at any time which allows learners to get a head start on the course.
  • Stepik developers engage in training and projects relating to accessibility that both educate and improve the accessibility of our products as they are being developed.
  • Stepik manages an email alias where incoming accessibility support tickets from learners are addressed
Any questions or requests about Accessibility should be addressed to support@stepik.org.
