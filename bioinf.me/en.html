ABOUT
Bioinformatics Institute is non-governmental non-profit Bioinformatics research and educational institution. It operates since 2010 with support from JetBrains company. The Institute's research division was launched in 2015.

The purpose of educational programs is to acquaint students with the main objectives and methods of bioinformatics and with open problems related to this science.

It is currently located in Boston, USA.
