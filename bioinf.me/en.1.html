Bioinformatics Institute
Our mission is to educate new generation of highly skilled professionals and popularize Bioinformatics in Russia.
ABOUT
Bioinformatics Institute is non-governmental non-profit Bioinformatics research and educational institution. It operates since 2010 with initial support from JetBrains and St. Petersburg Academic University of the Russian Academy of Sciences. It is located in St. Petersburg, Russia. The Institute's research division was launched in 2015.

The purpose of educational programs is to acquaint students with the main objectives and methods of bioinformatics and with open problems related to this science.
