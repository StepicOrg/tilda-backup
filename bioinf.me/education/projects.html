Projects descriptions
Since the main scientific language in the modern world is English, the page of research projects is completely translated.

During their studies at the Bioinformatics Institute, each student is involved in a research project. Leading specialists from Russian and foreign scientific laboratories and companies in the field of bioinformatics and biotechnologies manage the projects.

If you have interesting tasks in bioinformatics for students, write to us at info@bioinf.me. The project includes weekly meetings with the manager in person or online, as well as 5-15 hours a week of independent work. Many projects result in further cooperation with students.

Many students add these links to their CVs to demonstrate bioinformatics processing experience and skills when applying for a job or science position.
