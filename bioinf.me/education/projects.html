Projects descriptions
Since the main scientific language nowadays is English, this page is intentionally left in English only
During their studies at the Bioinformatics Institute, each student is involved in a research project. Leading specialists from scientific laboratories and companies in the field of bioinformatics, biotechnology, or biostatistics supervise their research work.

The project includes weekly online meetings and 5-15 hours a week of independent student work. Many projects result in further cooperation and publications.

If your lab has tasks in bioinformatics for students, drop us a line at info@bioinf.me.
