Hyperskill is an online platform for project-based learning that helps individuals and organizations to learn Computer Science (CS) and fill in the gaps in knowledge of STEM disciplines.

We strongly believe in accessible and practice-oriented education. Our mission is to provide people with different backgrounds an opportunity to learn new skills and change their life for the better.

Together with JetBrains, we are developing JetBrains Academy – a learning resource for future developers integrated with JetBrains IDEs. Hyperskill combines effective learning methods, a convenient platform, and an engaging curriculum, providing our learners with the best online education in CS and beyond.
