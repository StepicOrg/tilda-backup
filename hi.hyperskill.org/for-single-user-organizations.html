FAQ
What will happen to my learning progress if I join the organizational subscription with my personal account?
Your will keep your progress. Only the subscription type will change.
Will my employer see my activities on the platofrm?
Yes. The owner of the organizational account can track the learners' progress.
Can the owner of an organizational subscription study on the platform?
No, if there is just one learner's seat in the subscription. They have to buy more seats and use one.
I already have a personal subscription. Can I upgrade it to an organizational one?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan anytime. You have to cancel your personal subscription after switching to the organizational one. Please contact JetBrains Sales Support Team to find out about the refund. Recalculation is not possible.

I still have questions about the organizational subscription. Can you help me?
Sure! Please contact us at sales@hyperskill.org. One of our managers will be happy to help.
